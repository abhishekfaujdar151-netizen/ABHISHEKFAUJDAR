About PDFMaster

We're on a mission to make professional PDF tools accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Who We Are

PDFMaster is a leading online platform providing free, professional-grade PDF tools to millions of users worldwide. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a simple merge tool into a comprehensive suite of 30+ document solutions.

Our team of engineers and designers is passionate about creating tools that are powerful yet beautifully simple — no downloads, no complexity, just results.

By the Numbers

10M+ files processed monthly

180+ countries served · 30+ tools · 99.9% uptime

Our Mission

To democratize document productivity by providing fast, secure, and free PDF tools that empower individuals and businesses to work smarter — without barriers of cost, platform, or technical skill.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted platform for online document management — where every person can edit, convert, and share PDFs with confidence, privacy, and ease.

Why Choose PDFMaster?

  • Free core tools — No paywall for essential PDF operations
  • Enterprise-grade security — 256-bit SSL and automatic file deletion
  • Blazing fast — Optimized cloud infrastructure processes files in seconds
  • Works everywhere — Desktop, tablet, and mobile — any modern browser
  • Constant innovation — New tools and features added based on user feedback
